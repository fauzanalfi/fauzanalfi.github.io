This project broadens the research portfolio from building-scale workflows toward environmental and ecological representation. It explores how urban forest data can be organized in ways that support both analytical rigor and immersive interpretation.

The project sits at the intersection of data modeling, digital twin research, and spatial interfaces. It is especially relevant for future work on public-facing environmental visualization and interactive planning tools.
