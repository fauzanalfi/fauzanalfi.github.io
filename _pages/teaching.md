My teaching work connects technical workflows to architectural judgement. The emphasis is not only on tool use, but on how computation, immersive media, and model-based methods shape design thinking, communication, and review.

I treat teaching as a bridge between research and practice: students should leave with clearer methods, stronger conceptual framing, and a working understanding of how digital tools support decision-making.
